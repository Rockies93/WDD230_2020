Preston City Council urges Caution with Fireworks

4th of July is coming up so get ready for summer barbeques and fireworks. Due to this dryer than normal summer, the Preston City Council urges caution with fireworks. Please avoid using fireworks near brush or other flammable plant matter. Keep a bucket of weather nearby and follow basic firework safety standards. If you do not wish to do fireworks at your home, or if you live in a high risk area, please come to our city firework show at Center Park.
