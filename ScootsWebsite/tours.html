Mayan Ruins

The San Gervasio Ruins are pre-columbian mayan ruins on the northern side of Cozumel. Walk around the ruins and see sites such as Las Manitas, Chi Chan Nah, and el Arco. It’s a wonderful place to spend the day for history lovers!

Great Places to Snorkel

The waters off the coast are beautifully clear with turquoise waters, abundant sea life and visibility up to 200 ft. The most famous reefs to visit are Palancar Reef and Columbia Reef. There you can see turtles, exotic fish, and even some sharks!

Food and Drink

Is food more of your thing? Then buckle up because Cozumel is known for its delicious mexican take on seafood? Are you looking for some refreshing fish tacos? Ceviche? Or maybe just some late night cocktails? Cozumel has it all.

Natural Wonders

If you’re aching to see some of the natural wonders of Cozumel, maybe a trip to the Punta Sur Eco Beach Park would do you well. 2500 acres of nature reserve is home to birds, turtles,and crocodiles. You can explore it all on the many hiking trails. Once you’ve visited the parks, take some time to wander along the pristine beaches.
